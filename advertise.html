Advertising

About

SafeT is hosted on a web server that consumes electricity. As SafeT is a free service, we aim to recover some of the operating costs by selling advertising space to local businesses that are inclusive and accepting—determined based off of reviews from the community and other assessment factors.

Pricing

In order to remain inclusive for small businesses, SafeT charges a minimal fee per week for advertising. This is currently set at Â£1.00, though we expect this may go up as the platform's userbase grows. The next price review is scheduled on or after 30th September 2025.

Criteria

SafeT prioritises businesses that provide gender-affirming products for people who identify as trans and gender nonconforming, spaces that are explicitly inclusive to those from the LGBTQIA+ community, and organisations that provide direct support for LGBTQIA+ people.

Appearance

One caveat of advertising on SafeT is that our front-page banner advertisements will not appear on screens that are fewer than 850 pixels in width. This helps keep our website easily navigable on mobile devices, where people may need to access information quickly whilst still providing valuable reach on desktop and tablet devices.
