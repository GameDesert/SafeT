About

SafeT (pronounced safety) is a free community project designed to collate resources for people identifying as transgender and gender nonconforming who live, work, and study in London.

Disclaimer

Resources on this site are collated from and reference various sources, usually online, and thus are liable to being incorrect or outdated (most resources will be marked with a date of publishing)—though we do take reasonable precautions to ensure they are helpful and accurate. Information on this site will, where possible, have a link to the original source. No advice on this website shall constitute legal or medical advice, and you must carry out your own research and make your own informed decisions about anything you read here.

SafeT does not use AI to generate content or advice, as we are aware of the risks present regarding hallucinated/misleading content.

In the interest of transparency, please note that this website and its content is predominantly—but not exclusively—built and written by a cisgender member of the LGBTQIA+ community.
