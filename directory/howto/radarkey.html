Radar Key

About

The Radar key is a skeleton key of a common pattern distributed by disability rights organisations across the UK. It is intended for use by people with disabilities in order to access facilities that are otherwise kept locked to the general public. Src: Motability

Due to current societal attitudes and government policies, many trans and gender nonconforming people are unable to access gendered bathroom facilities and are thus compelled to use accessible bathrooms or avoid going to the toilet altogether.

You do not need to have a disability to buy, possess, or use a Radar key. SafeT discourages the use of the Radar key by people who can comfortably and safely use standard gendered bathrooms and we ask that people remain considerate of those who may require the use of an accessible facility more than themselves.

That being said, if an establishment refuses to provide a facility you can use comfortably and safely, there is nothing wrong with acquiring a Radar key and using it to access the facilities you need.

Purchase

SafeT is aware of several services online that sell affordable and low-profile (without the large heads for people with motor disabilities) Radar keys to UK customers. There is no commercial relationship between SafeT and any of these firms, nor can we speak to the quality of their goods. Please research responsibly before purchase.
